What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

J.C. Leyendecker is a very inspiring figure and hero to me, as he was such a brilliant artist and his courage as a gay man in the early 20th century is admirable. His artistic style and success not only shaped illustration in America, but also paved the way for future generations of LGBTQ+ artists.
